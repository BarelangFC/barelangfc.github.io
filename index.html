DIVISION

OUR DIVISION

We Have 6 Division

INTELLEGENT

The field of Intelligence is a field that creates and develops artificial intelligence in robots that are useful as brains in robots.

VISION

Vision is the field responsible for developing and implementing useful object detection as a sense of sight.

MECHANIC

The Mechanical part is the part that is in charge of making the robot design to the implementation of the actual robot form.

ELECTRICAL

The electrical division is in charge and responsible for planning and manufacturing the design of the entire electrical system.

MOTION

The Motion is the division whose job is to create and develop movements in robots, such as walking and kicking.

OFFICIAL

Official team as team manager, in charge and responsible for managing proposals, and administration.
